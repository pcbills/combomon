How to Play

Objective

Match Combomon to fulfill trainers' requests and unlock all 333 creatures in your Combodex!

How to Play

1. Read each trainer's request (e.g., "I want all Fire Combomon!")

2. Click a Combomon from the board, then click a slot to place it

3. Fill all three slots for each trainer with matching Combomon

4. Click "Submit Complete Teams" to check your answers

5. Correct matches unlock those Combomon in your Combodex

Difficulty Levels

Difficulty 1: Trainers want 1 matching attribute

Difficulty 2: Trainers want 2 matching attributes

Difficulty 3: Trainers want 3 matching attributes
